## 📌 Overview

**JobTrackPro** is a full-stack, AI-powered job search and application tracking platform designed exclusively for Northeastern University students. The platform unifies fragmented career tools by integrating directly with university systems (NUWorks & Workday), automating resume review using AI, and delivering real-time career guidance through a generative AI chatbot.

This project was planned, managed, and delivered end-to-end as a cross-functional team initiative under the **EMGT 5220 – Project Management** course, guided by Prof. Sharad Bajracharya.

---

## 🧩 Problem Statement

Northeastern University students faced a deeply fragmented job search experience:

- **~80%** reported difficulty tracking job applications across multiple platforms
- **~60%** cited limited and scattered job listings as a major pain point
- **25% of graduates** struggled to find jobs quickly due to lack of structured support
- No unified platform connected NUWorks, Workday, resume tools, and career guidance in one place

> *"Students face a complex job search process with limited personalized guidance, challenging application tracking, and lack of an accurate autofill feature — leading to frustration and a need for an integrated solution."*

---

## 💡 Solution

JobTrackPro delivers a **unified, intelligent platform** that:

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| 🔗 **System Integration** | Seamlessly connects with NUWorks & Workday via APIs and Puppeteer-based automation |
| 📄 **AI Resume Builder/Reviewer** | Rule-based scoring with 5 conditional rules + AI optimization |
| 📊 **Job Tracker Dashboard** | Application status tracking with reminders, dashboard display & data persistence |
| 🤖 **Generative AI Chatbot** | Real-time, personalized job search guidance powered by ChatGPT |
| ✏️ **Autofill Feature** | Smart profile-based autofill for faster application submission |

## 📋 My Role — Technical Program Manager

As the **Technical Program Manager**, I was responsible for end-to-end program delivery across a 7-person cross-functional team. Key contributions included:

### 📐 Program Planning & Execution
- Developed the full **Work Breakdown Structure (WBS)** decomposing all deliverables across 4 technical modules
- Built and maintained the **Gantt Chart schedule** tracking milestones from inception to project closure
- Created the **RACI Matrix** defining ownership across PM, Product, DevOps, QA, Cloud Arch, and UI/UX roles
- Led **sprint planning and agile ceremonies** ensuring iterative delivery aligned to user feedback

### 📊 Data-Driven Decision Making
- Conducted **market research via Google Forms** to quantify student pain points and prioritize features
- Analyzed survey data to produce the product roadmap and feature priority stack
- Tracked KPIs across module delivery, risk exposure, and budget consumption

### 💰 Financial Oversight
- Managed a total program budget of **$1,473,712** across labor, software, cloud services, and contingency
- Produced a **5-year NPV analysis** (NPV = ~$1.57M at 8% discount rate) demonstrating financial viability
- Built revenue model projecting **$722,000 in annual revenue** from subscriptions, university partnerships, advertising, and premium features

### ⚠️ Risk Management
- Identified, categorized, and maintained a **Risk Register** covering technical, operational, and external risks
- Applied a 15% contingency reserve ($192,223) against a combined development + operational base
- Implemented continuous risk assessment cadence with stakeholder escalation protocols

### 🤝 Stakeholder Management
- Managed relationships across 6 stakeholder groups: students, Career Services, external platforms (Workday, NUWorks), AI technology providers, potential employers, and the internal project team
- Presented project proposals, progress reviews, and final deliverables to faculty sponsors

### 🔒 Project Governance
- Defined **project monitoring** cadence using agile methodologies for Workday/NUWorks integration checkpoints
- Led **project control** process for scope change management, particularly for the AI Resume Builder
- Planned **independent audit** criteria for the Job Tracking Database and AI Chatbot compliance
- Delivered a formal **project closure report** with benchmarks for future platform enhancements
